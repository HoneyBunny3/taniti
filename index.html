Taniti Island

Taniti Island is a robust, 500sq mi island in the Pacific ocean. Your senses have never experienced such a variety of ecosystems:

Sandy beaches with natural, rocky landscape.

Exotic tropical rainforest, bountiful and thriving.

An active volcano surrounded by a gorgeous mountain range.
