Travel to Taniti

Travel to and around the Island!


    Our Safe Harbor

  • Arrive in style on our compact cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay, once every week.

  • Our harbor is a safe and well traveled port of transportation.

  • Used by our local fisherman and merchants, ths harbor is a crucial Tanitian landmark!


    • Fly with us!

  • Our compact airport is spacious enough for most small aircraft, propeller planes and helicopters.

  • We are projected to expand our airport to accomodate larger jets in the coming years.


  • Most of our visitors choose to fly!

How to get around the island like a local
  • Tour the island coast with a local guide. Take in the full majesty of the Tanitian landscape.

  • Fly over the Taniti Volcano and take in our gorgeous homeland from the sky.

  • Use our private busses to get around the city and commute to our neighboring city, Merriton Landing!

  • Tour the island on a local bus tour. See the sights and enjoy the local history with our local tour guides.

  • Our public busses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m., daily.

  • Taxis are available in Taniti City.

  • Rental cars are available from our local rental agency near the airport.

  • There are plenty of flat routes to walkable attractions no matter where you choose to stay, Taniti or Merriton Landing.

  • Bikes, and our law required helmets, are available to rent.
